Sample 3-Year Roadmap

What a structured quantum-safe migration looks like

1
Discovery & Planning Months 1-6
2
Pilot & Validate Months 6-12
3
Production Rollout Year 2-3

Phase 1: Discovery & Planning

  • Complete cryptographic inventory
  • Classify data by sensitivity period
  • Assess HSM/PKI capabilities
  • Engage critical vendors on PQC roadmaps
  • Develop detailed migration plan
  • Secure executive sponsorship

Phase 2: Pilot & Validate

  • Deploy hybrid PQC in test environments
  • Measure performance impacts
  • Train security and development teams
  • Update procurement requirements
  • Begin PKI/certificate updates
  • Validate vendor compatibility

Phase 3: Production Rollout

  • Roll out hybrid crypto to production
  • Upgrade HSMs and key management
  • Implement crypto agility framework
  • Establish ongoing monitoring
  • Regular board progress reporting
  • Prepare for full PQC transition
