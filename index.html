Physical vs Logical Qubits

Why "1 million qubits" headlines are misleading

~500 physical qubits with error correction

Physical Qubits

  • Actual quantum bits in hardware
  • ~1% error rate (very high)
  • Lose quantum properties quickly (decoherence)
  • Current systems: 50-1,000 physical qubits

Logical Qubits

  • Virtual qubits from many physical qubits
  • Error-corrected and reliable
  • Required for cryptographically-relevant computation
  • Current bottleneck: ~500 physical = 1 logical
"When you read '1 million physical qubits achieved,' remember: that's roughly 2,000 logical qubits. Breaking RSA-2048 requires approximately 4,000 logical qubits running Shor's algorithm."
